About movie

Just when things seem happy and normal in the life of 38 year old Prabhu, his wife Bhuvana and 12 year old daughter Sathya,

Prabhu`s situation in life makes him reconnect with his brother, Kathir, who is a skilled hunter and has an uncanny resemblance to Prabhu.
Will Prabhu be able to rekindle the long lost bond with his brother? Will Prabhu`s daughter Sathya`s health improve?
